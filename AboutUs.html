About Carleton Cupcakes


Carleton Cupcakes is a fun bakery shop that loves the online shopping trend!

Here, we believe that quality bakery items found on the web can also find their way to your doorstep when you want it. Inspired by the websites for the dessert shops of Ottawa, we decided to take them as inspiration for Carleton Cupcakes.

It was a true interest that sparked the creation of such a finger-licking good food company. The idea of offering such delicious treats with much needed convenience - need we say more? Joining the never-ending paths on the World Wide Web and leaving behind a trail of yummy crumbs from our great recipes is what we aim to show.
